China’s Bulk Drug Procurement Program Hits Quality Snags as Regulators Oust Suppliers

Caixin Global | FULLTEXT | 675 words | 2026-06-29 16:41

Shandong Beida Gaoke Huatai Pharmaceutical Co.

Shandong Beida Gaoke Huatai Pharmaceutical Co. had its production line for amiodarone hydrochloride injection suspended due to serious deficiencies. Its winning-bid status was revoked, and it was barred from centralized drug procurement from June 9, 2026, to Dec. 8, 2027.

Harbin Liqiang Pharmaceutical Co.

Harbin Liqiang Pharmaceutical Co. was stripped of its winning-bid status in China's national drug procurement program due to serious defects found in its loxoprofen sodium gel patch production line. The company was placed on a violation list.

Chengdu Tiantaishan Pharmaceutical Co.

Chengdu Tiantaishan Pharmaceutical Co. was disqualified from China's national drug procurement program after its methocarbamol injection was found to have serious production defects. The company manufactured the drug on behalf of Guangzhou Hehe Pharmaceutical Co., and its winning-bid status was revoked due to quality management issues.

Guangzhou Hehe Pharmaceutical Co.

Guangzhou Hehe Pharmaceutical Co. was involved in a quality case where methocarbamol injection made by Chengdu Tiantaishan Pharmaceutical on its behalf was disqualifed from China's national drug procurement program. Regulators stripped its winning-bid status due to serious production defects, and the company was placed on a violation list.

Weifang Zhongshi Pharmaceutical Co.

Weifang Zhongshi Pharmaceutical Co. was disqualified from China's drug procurement program after inspectors found serious production defects. Its formoterol fumarate inhalation solution, produced for Chongqing Derunsheng Pharmaceutical Co., lost its winning-bid status. The company was placed on a violation list and barred from bidding.

Chongqing Derunsheng Pharmaceutical Co.

Chongqing Derunsheng Pharmaceutical Co. was disqualified from China's centralized drug procurement after its commissioned product, formoterol fumarate inhalation solution made by Weifang Zhongshi Pharmaceutical, was found to have serious production defects. The company lost its winning-bid status as part of broader regulatory crackdowns on quality issues.

Hunan Weidekang Medical Technology Co.

Hunan Weidekang Medical Technology Co. was a winning bidder in China's sixth round of centralized high-value medical consumables procurement but was later disqualified. Regulators found serious defects in its quality-management system, failing to comply with medical-device production quality-management rules. The company's disqualification underscores rising safety concerns in China's bulk-procurement system.

Hunan Yingshu Life Technology Co.

Hunan Yingshu Life Technology Co. was a winning bidder in China's sixth round of centralized procurement for high-value medical consumables but was disqualified after regulators found serious defects in its quality-management system, including non-compliance with medical-device production rules. Products involved included ureteral access guidewires and balloon dilation catheters.

Hunan Keyi Zhitou Biotechnology Co.

Hunan Keyi Zhitou Biotechnology Co. was a winning bidder in China's sixth round of centralized procurement for high-value medical consumables. Regulators found serious defects in its quality management system, failing to comply with medical-device production rules. Products involved included ureteral access guidewires and ureteral balloon dilation catheters.

Hunan Tongpu Kangye Medical Technology Co.

Hunan Tongpu Kangye Medical Technology Co. was a winning bidder in China's sixth round of centralized procurement for high-value medical consumables. Regulators found serious defects in its quality-management system, violating production rules, leading to disqualification.

Hunan Zhongju Endoscope Co.

Hunan Zhongju Endoscope Co., a winning bidder in China's sixth round of centralized procurement for high-value medical consumables, was disqualified due to serious quality-management system defects. Regulators found the company failed to comply with medical-device production quality rules, affecting products including ureteral access guidewires and dilation catheters.

Shenzhen Taimeikang Biomedical Technology Co.

The company failed to comply with medical-device production quality rules for products including ureteral access guidewires and balloon dilation catheters.
