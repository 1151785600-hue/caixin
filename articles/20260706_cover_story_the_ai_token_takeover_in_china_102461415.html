Caixin Global | FULLTEXT | 400 words | 2026-07-09 11:56

December 2024：

Average daily token throughput on SiliconFlow platform was 47.8 billion.

Before 2025：

In the early days of AI chatbots, a typical user interaction involved only a few hundred Chinese characters.

2025：

Nvidia Corp. and the world’s three major memory-chip makers generated more than $160 billion in combined operating profit.

2025：

Revenue of SiliconFlow reached 55.3 million yuan, up more than 650% from a year earlier.

2025：

SiliconFlow ranked fourth in China’s Model-as-a-Service market by token call volume.

August 2025：

A wave of data-center construction began, tightening supply of memory chips, graphics processors, central processors, optical modules and liquid-cooling systems.

By end of 2025：

Kingsoft Office said WPS AI had more than 80.1 million monthly active users in China, up 307% from a year earlier, and generated more than 200 billion daily token calls, up more than twelvefold.

First quarter of 2026：

Zhipu AI's API revenue rose sixtyfold from a year earlier.

First quarter of 2026：

Zhipu AI CEO Zhang Peng said Zhipu’s API prices rose 83% quarter-on-quarter, while call volume still jumped more than fourfold.

March 2026：

Tencent Cloud raised prices for core models by more than 430%.

March 2026：

Alibaba Cloud said it would raise prices for some AI computing services by 5% to 34% and increase the price of its high-performance storage system by 30%.

March 2026：

Tencent’s WorkBuddy launched publicly.

March 2026：

WorkBuddy ranked first among China’s PC-based AI-native office-agent platforms with 8.85 million monthly visits and 831% month-on-month user growth.

April 2026：

Average daily token throughput on SiliconFlow platform rose to 578.5 billion.

May 2026：

DeepSeek cut prices again; its DeepSeek-V4-Pro was priced at one-quarter of its original level.

May 20, 2026：

MuleRun launched overseas and within two months had paying users in 43 countries.

June 2026：

ByteDance’s Doubao model surpassed 180 trillion token calls a day.

June 2026：

ByteDance’s Feishu connected its agent Aily to workplace scenarios.

June 16, 2026：

SiliconFlow completed a Series B financing round of more than 2 billion yuan.

June 17, 2026：

A report by Tongdao Liepin Group and Tsinghua University’s Center for AI and Management Research showed demand for AI algorithm and model development talent fell from about 50% in 2022 to 20% in the first quarter of 2026.

June 23, 2026：

ByteDance released Doubao 2.1, doubling the price from the previous version.

June 25, 2026：

DeepSeek posted a recruitment notice reflecting a shift toward AI product and operations roles.

Late June 2026：

DeepSeek said it would launch the official version of DeepSeek-V4 in mid-July 2026 and introduce peak-and-valley pricing.
