Meituan Open-Sources 1.6-Trillion-Parameter AI Model Built on Chinese Chips

Caixin Global | FULLTEXT | 368 words | 2026-07-13 12:02

Meituan

Meituan, a Chinese tech giant known for its food delivery platform, open-sourced the LongCat-2.0 AI model with 1.6 trillion parameters, trained entirely on domestic chips. This demonstrates innovation amid U.S. export controls. The company accelerated AI efforts after acquiring Light Year in 2023 and increased R&D spending by 22% in early 2026.

Huawei Technologies Co.

Huawei Technologies Co. is a Chinese semiconductor firm whose Ascend Atlas A2 chip has 64 GB memory per chip, compared to Nvidia's H100 with up to 96 GB. Huawei confirmed its hardware supports Meituan's open-sourced LongCat-2.0 AI model, trained on domestic chips under U.S. export controls.

Moore Threads

Moore Threads is a Chinese semiconductor firm that manufactures AI processors. It confirmed that its hardware supports Meituan's LongCat-2.0 model, which was trained on domestic chips. This highlights the company's role in advancing China's AI capabilities amid U.S. export controls.

MetaX

MetaX is a Chinese semiconductor company that confirmed its hardware supports Meituan's 1.6-trillion-parameter AI model, LongCat-2.0, trained entirely on domestic chips. It is one of several local firms enabling innovation despite U.S. export controls on advanced processors.

Nvidia Corp.

Nvidia Corp.'s flagship H100 GPU has up to 96 gigabytes of memory per chip, outperforming Chinese alternatives like Huawei's 64-gigabyte Ascend Atlas A2. U.S. export controls have spurred Chinese firms to innovate with domestic chips, but Nvidia remains a leader in memory capacity and interconnect bandwidth.

OpenAI

Based on the article, Meituan's LongCat-2.0 AI model outperformed OpenAI's GPT-5.5 in programming and mathematical reasoning benchmarks. No other information about OpenAI is provided in the article.

Anthropic

In the article, Anthropic's AI models Claude Opus 4.6 and 4.8 are referenced. Meituan's LongCat-2.0 outperformed Claude Opus 4.6 in programming and math but lags behind Claude Opus 4.8 in multi-disciplinary research and complex scenarios.

Light Year

In 2023, Meituan acquired the AI startup Light Year for $285 million. This acquisition accelerated Meituan's AI development, leading to the release of the LongCat-2.0 model trained on domestic chips.

OpenRouter

According to the article, OpenRouter is an API routing platform where an early preview of Meituan's LongCat-2.0 model, "Owl Alpha," became one of the most widely used free systems globally.
