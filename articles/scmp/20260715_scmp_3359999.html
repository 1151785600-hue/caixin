8 Hong Kong teens arrested over taking cash from gang to splash paint on doors

SCMP | FREE | 324 words | 2026-07-15

Eight teenagers have been arrested after allegedly being recruited via social media by a crime syndicate to splash paint on the doors of flats and businesses across Hong Kong to intimidate debtors.

The four boys and four girls were apprehended alongside a 24-year-old man on suspicion of criminal damage and conspiracy to commit criminal damage as part of a citywide police operation on Tuesday and Wednesday.

The force said on Thursday that a crime syndicate had used popular social media platforms and messaging apps to recruit young people with the promise of quick cash, offering rewards ranging from HK$800 to HK$2,000 (US$102 to US$255) per assignment.

“[The syndicate] assigned them tasks, which included purchasing red paint and splashing it at specific targets,” the force said.

In Hong Kong, splashing paint on doors is commonly used as an intimidation tactic by criminal groups collecting debts.

Three of the suspects – a 14-year-old girl, a 15-year-old boy and the man – were allegedly members of a triad-linked debt-collection syndicate that recruited people online to splash paint.

The six remaining teenagers, aged between 12 and 16, were believed to have been hired by the trio to carry out the vandalism.

Police said the man had told officers he was a fitness coach, while the teenagers were either students or had dropped out of school.

The force opened an investigation after receiving four reports between late May and mid-June of red paint being splashed on the fronts of residential and commercial properties in Tsim Sha Tsui and Yau Ma Tei.

Investigators later linked the suspects to at least two similar incidents in Kwun Tong and Tin Shui Wai that took place in June.

During the operation, officers seized red paint and clothing believed to have been worn by the suspects at the time of the offences.

All of the suspects have been released on bail and are required to report back to police in mid-August.
